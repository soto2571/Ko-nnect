Our Features!

Made to Perfection for Employees and Admins

Effortless Schedule Creation

Admins and managers can easily create and assign schedules for employees, minimizing scheduling conflicts and enhancing team communication.

Real-Time Schedule Access

Employees can log in from their phone or computer to view their schedules instantly, ensuring they stay informed about upcoming shifts and avoid miscommunication.

Holiday and Shift Calendar

View a calendar of shifts up to 3 weeks in advance, including public holidays in Puerto Rico. Stay informed about workdays and upcoming days off.

Customizable Employee Access

Admins create unique credentials for each employee, allowing them to securely access their schedules, view shift details, and receive real-time updates.

Two-Week Schedule View

Employees can scroll through a list of their current and upcoming shifts over the next 14 days, providing a convenient alternative to the calendar view.

Admin Dashboard

A centralized dashboard for admins to manage employee accounts, view schedules, and ensure everything is organized in one easy-to-access location.
